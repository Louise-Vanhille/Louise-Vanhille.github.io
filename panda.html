Panda Pootjes

For this project, I designed a cookie box called Panda Pootjes. It’s filled with chocolate cookies. The little mascot is a purple (red) panda. On the back, there’s a little surprise: can you spot the 7 pandas? I loved illustrating this cutie! I had so much fun coming up with this concept. I hope you love it too!
